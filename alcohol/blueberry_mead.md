Blueberry mead recipe (5L - 15%)

## ingredients

- 1.2kg blueberries
- 1.2kg honey
- 5g hetvingjær 
- tiny bit of breadyeast
- 4,5 L water

## instructions
- freeze and thaw the blueberries
- boil a bottom of water with the breadyeast
- put the berries in a strainer nylon bag
- smash the blueberries
- add more water, untill it's 4 liters
- once it's cooled down below 27*C, add the honey
- pour it into the fermentation vessel
- add the hetvingjær and shake for 3min
- let ferment for 6 weeks ish
- seal in bottles, and backsweeten if needed
