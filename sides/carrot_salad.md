# Pickled carrot salad recipe

## Ingredients

* **4 cups** carrots, peeled and thinly sliced or shredded
* **1/2 cup** white vinegar or apple cider vinegar
* **1/4 cup** water
* **2 tablespoons** sugar
* **1 teaspoon** salt
* **1/2 teaspoon** black pepper
* **1 tablespoon** olive oil
* **1** small red onion, thinly sliced
* **2 cloves** garlic, minced
* **1 tablespoon** fresh lemon juice
* **2 tablespoons** fresh parsley or cilantro, chopped
* **1/2 teaspoon** red pepper flakes (optional)

## Instructions

1. **Prepare Carrots:**

   * Peel the carrots and slice them thinly, shred them, or cut them into matchsticks.
   * Place the carrots in a large bowl.

2. **Make Pickling Liquid:**

   * In a small saucepan, combine the vinegar, water, sugar, salt, and black pepper.
   * Heat over medium heat until the sugar and salt dissolve.
   * Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.

3. **Add Vegetables:**

   * Add the sliced red onion and minced garlic to the bowl with the carrots.
   * Pour the warm pickling liquid over the carrots and mix well.

4. **Chill Salad:**

   * Cover the bowl and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes.
   * For stronger flavor, let it chill for 1-2 hours.

5. **Finish Salad:**

   * Drain a little of the excess pickling liquid if needed.
   * Add olive oil, lemon juice, fresh parsley or cilantro, and red pepper flakes if using.
   * Toss everything together until well combined.

6. **Serve:**

   * Taste and adjust with more salt, pepper, or lemon juice if desired.
   * Serve chilled or at room temperature.
   * Enjoy!
