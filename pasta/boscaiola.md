# Pasta boscaiola recipe


## Ingredients

- rigatoni
- pancetta
- peas
- mushrooms
- onions


## Instructions

1. **Boil the water**
Add one spoon of salt to 1 liter of water and start boiling the water.

2. **Cock rigatoni**
Cook the rigatoni for 12 minutes in boiling water.

3. ** Make the sauce **
While cooking the ragatoni, brown your finely choped onions and then add peas, mushrooms and pancetta.
