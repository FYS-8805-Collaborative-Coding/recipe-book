# Pasta tomato recipe

## Ingredients

- pasta
- canned tomatoes
- sugar
- salt
- garlic
- basil
- olive oil

## Instructions

- Boil some water for the pasta
- Oil the pan
- Heat the canned tomatoes in the pan and mash them
- Add a bit of salt
- Add the whole garlic
- Add basil
- In parallel cook the pasta in the boiling water
- Add sugar and let it simmer for at least 5 minutes, the more the better if it doesn't burn.
- Taste and adjust the salt if necessary
- Add the cooked pasta to the pan and mix them map under low heat.
- Enjoy !!
