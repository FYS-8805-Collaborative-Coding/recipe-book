# Spaghetti carbonara recipe

## Ingredients

* **12 oz** spaghetti
* **4 oz** pancetta or guanciale, diced
* **2** large eggs
* **1 cup** grated Pecorino Romano cheese
* **1/2 cup** grated Parmesan cheese
* **2-3 cloves** garlic, lightly crushed (optional)
* **Salt and black pepper** to taste
* **1 tablespoon** olive oil, if needed
* **Fresh parsley** for garnish (optional)

## Instructions

1. **Cook Pasta:**

   * Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
   * Add the spaghetti and cook until al dente according to package instructions.
   * Reserve **1 cup** of pasta water, then drain the spaghetti.

2. **Cook Pancetta or Guanciale:**

   * Heat a large skillet over medium heat.
   * Add the diced pancetta or guanciale and cook until crispy and golden.
   * Add the crushed garlic if using, then remove it once fragrant.

3. **Mix Eggs and Cheese:**

   * In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, Pecorino Romano cheese, Parmesan cheese, and plenty of black pepper.
   * Set aside.

4. **Combine Pasta and Meat:**

   * Add the hot spaghetti to the skillet with the pancetta or guanciale.
   * Toss well so the pasta is coated in the rendered fat.

5. **Make the Sauce:**

   * Remove the skillet from heat.
   * Pour the egg and cheese mixture over the pasta, tossing quickly.
   * Add reserved pasta water a little at a time until the sauce becomes creamy.

6. **Serve:**

   * Season with extra black pepper and more grated cheese if desired.
   * Garnish with fresh parsley if using.
   * Serve warm and enjoy!
